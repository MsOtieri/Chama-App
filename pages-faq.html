Basic Questions
1. What is Chama about?

Chama was started as a simple table banking application to help make people in the society more accountable and responsible for their finances.

2. How does Chama work?

It is pretty simple to use this web application since the steps are easy to follow. If you follow the instructions step by step, you should be able to understand how to navigate through it.

3. Do I need to do anything specific to join chama?

Yes, once you are accepted as a member, you will need to make deposits to our account for you to get your shares at the end of each month and make it possible to get a loan when you are in need.

About Chama

Chama is a simple table banking web application that was developed to help the finacial status of the society. This will help people to avoid poverty by securing financial help from their community to become self-employed.

This application has the following features to ease navigation through it, the user registration where new members sign up and current members login, Account management where the admin is profiled for all memebers to see, Transaction updates in realtime, messages and notifications are available to all members, individual transaction history for each member, and up-to-date reports by the treasurer.

Chama App ensures user authentication, where the user has access to their personal data on their own.The app also ensures backups incase a member loses their membership access to the website, and by use of anti-fraud mechanisms, the app prevents fraud. The app also complies with data protection laws and regulations to protect user privacy.

Chama has its email where members can raise their concerns if they are not comfortable with the public chart. There is a responsive support team that answers concerns raised. There is user feedback where necessary. There are continuous updates on the transactions made by members to their accounts in realtime.

Chama wishes to be globally recognized and usable to help people manage their finances well. To make improvements on its general functionality by improving the user experience and integrating with mobile money platforms to make it easier for all members to use these services.
Personal information

Members of Chama are allowed to make a change to their name, profile picture, password, phone number, and email

Chama has the profile section where members can make changes to their personal information whenever they want to and allow the database to fetch this information for verification.

Once a member has entered all the details they want to change in their profile, there is a submit button where they must press to allow the database fetch and save the new details and make an update.

The updates are made almost immediately as long as the user submits their new details and the database fetches the details.

Using the available contact information in the website, that is, the email, the user should contact the support team for assistance in updating their personal information to what they feel comfortable with.
